F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful reference that provides answers to common queries and resolves typical issues efficiently.

  • How to get to Siargao?

    To reach Siargao, book a domestic flight to Sayak Airport from Cebu. If you prefer a scenic route, you can optionally take a ferry from Surigao City. Once you arrive, make arrangements for local transportation to your accommodation.

  • When is the best time to go to Siargao?

    The best time to visit Siargao is during the dry season from March to October, with June to August being the peak season. It's ideal for sunny weather and outdoor activities. Avoid the wet season from November to February when rain is more frequent.

  • What are the must-do activities in Siargao?

    In Siargao, you must surf at Cloud 9, go island hopping to Naked Island, Guyam Island, and Daku Island, explore Sugba Lagoon, visit the unique Magpupungko Rock Pools, and enjoy the vibrant nightlife in General Luna.

  • Where to stay in Siargao?

    Popular areas to stay in Siargao include General Luna, Cloud 9, and nearby villages like Pacifico and Dapa. These areas offer a range of accommodations, from hostels to luxury resorts, to suit different budgets and preferences.

  • How much does it cost to travel to Siargao?

    A budget traveler in Siargao might spend around 1,500 to 2,500 Philippine Pesos per day, while a mid-range traveler could budget for 2,500 to 5,000 Philippine Pesos per day. For luxury travelers, the daily expenses could exceed 5,000 Philippine Pesos. Domestic flights typically range from 7,500 to 16,000 Philippine Pesos round trip, depending on your departure location and booking timing.

  • What is the currency in Siargao?

    The currency used in Siargao, Philippines, is the Philippine Peso (PHP).

  • Is it safe to travel to Siargao?

    Siargao is generally safe for travelers. However, exercise common travel precautions, such as securing belongings and being mindful of the weather and sea conditions. Stay updated on any local advisories and health recommendations.

  • What are the visa requirements for Siargao?

    Most tourists can enter the Philippines without a visa for 30 days. If needed, you can extend your stay. Check the latest visa rules and ensure your passport is valid for at least six months beyond your intended departure date.

  • How can I get around in Siargao?

    You can get around Siargao by using tricycles, habal-habal (motorcycle taxis), rented scooters or bicycles, and vans or private vehicles.

  • What is the weather like in Siargao?

    Siargaoo has a tropical climate with a wet season from November to February and a dry season from March to October. The best time to visit is during the dry season for sunny weather.
